About Us

Studlancer is a platform for students to offer or request work from other students, all for fair payment. Whether you need help with assignments, projects, or freelance work, Studlancer brings students together to collaborate and succeed.

How It Works

  1. Sign up and create your profile.
  2. Post a task or browse available tasks.
  3. Negotiate and agree on a fair price for the work.
  4. Complete the task and receive payment securely.
